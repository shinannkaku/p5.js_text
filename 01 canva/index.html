我對於資料科學和資訊視覺化相當有興趣，喜歡研究R語言和Python等程式設計以及政府開放資料的應用。此外，我對網站規劃與設計的方法與技術也有涉獵。

I am very interested in topics of data science and information visualization. I like to study R and Python programming, as well as the application of open government data. In addition, I also study the methods and techniques about website planning and design.
